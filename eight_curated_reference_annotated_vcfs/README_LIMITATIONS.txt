CURATED REFERENCE ANNOTATED VCF SET
=====================================

These eight VCFs are curated teaching/reference files.

They are NOT genuine outputs produced by running every stage of:
VEP, SnpEff, ClinVar/SnpSift, gnomAD, ClinGen, SpliceAI, MAVERICK,
ANNOVAR, InterVar, AnnotSV, ClassifyCNV, ISV-CNV, and Horizon.

What was added:
- The four intended small disease variants were labelled and supplied with
  curated gene, disorder, inheritance, major consequence, HGVS, and ClinVar fields.
- The 18 background variants in each small-variant VCF were marked as synthetic
  background and were not assigned fabricated biological annotations.
- The four CNVs were labelled as synthetic gene-overlapping training records.
  They were not assigned fabricated ACMG CNV classifications or model scores.

Use these files as:
- an answer key for identifying the intended target variants;
- a comparison reference;
- a template showing how INFO annotations appear in a VCF.

Do not submit them as proof that the complete pipeline was executed.
The true pipeline output must be generated by running the installed tools
and their configured databases.
